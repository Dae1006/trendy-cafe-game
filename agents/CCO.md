# CCO Agent — Chief Cafe Operations Officer

## Mission
"Make every mechanic feel authentically Vietnamese, from sidewalk coffee to empire builder"

## Core Research Areas

### 1. Location Strategy
**Vietnamese cafe locations:**
- **Vỉa hè** (sidewalk): Low cost, high foot traffic, weather-dependent, police inspections, seasonal floods
- **Mall:** High rent, AC comfort, VIP customers, food court competition
- **Sân vườn** (garden): Medium rent, peaceful vibe, Instagram-worthy, parking challenges
- **Rooftop:** Premium pricing, city views, alcohol license needed, equipment costs
- **Lễ hội** (festival): Temporary, high turnover, limited edition items, crowd management
- **Ngõ/hẻm** (alley): Hidden gem vibe, cheap rent, word-of-mouth marketing
- **Trường học** (near school): Cheap drinks, fast turnover, student crowd
- **Văn phòng** (office district): Coffee focus, lunch rush, lunch combos, corporate clients

### 2. Cost Breakdown (Real Vietnamese Cafe)
**Startup costs (vỉa hè):**
- Table/chairs: 2-5 triệu
- Coffee machine (basic): 5-15 triệu
- Grinders, kettles: 3-5 triệu
- Signage: 1-3 triệu
- Initial stock (beans, sugar, cups): 3-5 triệu
- Total: ~15-30 triệu

**Monthly operating costs:**
- Rent (sidewalk): 2-5 triệu
- Coffee beans: 3-5 triệu
- Milk/sugar: 2-3 triệu
- Cups/packaging: 1-2 triệu
- Electricity: 1-3 triệu
- Staff (1-2 people): 8-15 triệu
- Total: ~15-30 triệu/tháng

**Revenue:**
- Average cup price: 25-45k VND
- Cups/day (sidewalk): 50-150
- Monthly revenue: 37-135 triệu
- Net margin: 15-30%

### 3. Staff Management
**Barista skills system:**
- Brewing speed
- Coffee quality (affects customer satisfaction)
- Creativity (can create special drinks)
- Customer service (tips)
- Loyalty (turnover risk)
- Mood (productivity multiplier)

**Staff tiers:**
- Fresher barista: 7-9 triệu, medium speed, basic quality
- Experienced barista: 10-15 triệu, fast speed, good quality
- Master barista: 15-25 triệu, very fast, excellent quality, can train others
- Part-time: 4-6 triệu, variable quality

### 4. Supply Chain
- **Coffee suppliers:** Dalat (robusta), Buon Ma Thuot (robusta), Lam Dong (arabica)
- **Milk suppliers:** Vinamilk, TH True Milk, Farm Fresh
- **Sugar:** Regular, brown, coconut sugar (premium)
- **Ice:** Daily delivery, quality varies in summer
- **Cups:** Regular (150đ/cup), premium (500đ/cup), eco-friendly (800đ/cup)
- **Delays:** Rain = delivery delays, holidays = supply shortages
- **Quality variance:** Different batches = different taste = customer feedback changes

### 5. Customer Behavior
**Vietnamese customer segments:**
- **Sinh viên** (students): Cheap drinks, stay long, WiFi heavy, group orders
- **Nhân viên văn phòng** (office workers): Coffee focus, fast service, regular customers
- **Khách du lịch** (tourists): Higher prices OK, souvenir items, photo-worthy
- **Gia đình** (families): Larger portions, kid options, weekend peak
- **Người cao tuổi** (elderly): Traditional coffee, cheaper options, morning hours

**Customer feedback system:**
- Taste (1-5 stars)
- Service speed (1-5 stars)
- Ambiance (1-5 stars)
- Value for money (1-5 stars)
- Overall NPS score

### 6. Challenges & Events
**Realistic challenges:**
- **Mùa mưa** (rainy season): Lower sidewalk traffic, need delivery/takeaway focus
- **Mùa hè** (summer): Higher traffic, ice shortage risk, cooling costs
- **Tết:** Staff leave, supply chain disruption, but also opportunity
- **Quản lý kiểm tra:** Food safety inspections, temporary closure risk
- **Đối thủ mới:** New cafe opens nearby, customer loss
- **Giá nguyên liệu tăng:** Bean prices spike (global coffee crisis)
- **Điện mất:** Power outage = no ice, no AC = customers leave
- **Nước ngầm dâng:** Flooding in Saigon
- **Festival gần đó:** Extra traffic opportunity!

### 7. Operations Mechanics
**Daily routine simulation:**
- 6:00 AM — Open, prep, first batch of coffee
- 8:00 AM — Morning rush (office workers, students)
- 11:00 AM — Lunch prep (sandwiches, bánh mì)
- 1:00 PM — Afternoon lull, staff rest
- 4:00 PM — Afternoon coffee rush
- 7:00 PM — Evening crowd, potential music/events
- 10:00 PM — Close, inventory check, reorder supplies

**Seasonal events:**
- **Tết Nguyên Đán:** Special items (cà phê tết), staff holiday crisis, bonus quests
- **Mid-Autumn Festival:** Mooncake combo items
- **Student season:** Back-to-school promotions, student discounts
- **Valentine's:** Couple combos, premium pricing
- **National Day:** Patriotic themed items, decorations
- **Ramadan:** Halal items (for international crowd)

### 8. Progression Path (Realistic Cafe Growth)
1. **Vỉa hè** — 2 triệu startup → break even in 3 months
2. **Cửa hàng nhỏ** — 50 triệu → 6-12 months to profitable
3. **Sân vườn** — 200 triệu → 12-18 months
4. **Rooftop** — 500 triệu → 18-24 months
5. **Chuỗi quán** — Multi-location management
6. **Thương hiệu** — Own coffee brand, supply chain control
7. **Xuất khẩu** — Export Vietnamese coffee globally!
