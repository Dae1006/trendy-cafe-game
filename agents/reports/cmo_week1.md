# CMO Weekly Report — Week 1 (2026-05-09 to 2026-05-15)

## 🔥 Top 5 Trends to Reflect in Game

### 1. "Boba aesthetics" — Trà sữa vẫn cực hot 🔥
**Trend:** Bubble tea culture continues to dominate VN social media. #BubbleTea has billions of views on TikTok.
**Action:** Add new "Specialty Tea" location type. Add premium tea recipes. Create "Boba Festival" limited-time event.

### 2. "Cà phê sạch" — Organic/Sustainable coffee 🌿
**Trend:** Vietnamese consumers increasingly care about coffee origin and sustainability.
**Action:** Add organic coffee bean supplier tier. Add "eco-friendly" cups and decor items.

### 3. "Check-in sống ảo" 📸
**Trend:** Vietnamese customers still prioritize Instagrammable spots. Green walls, LED lights, neon signs.
**Action:** Add more photo-worthy decor items. Add "check-in score" metric that attracts more customers.

### 4. "Cozy café" movement 🕯️
**Trend:** Post-pandemic, people want cozy, warm spaces. Think fairy lights, wooden furniture, plants.
**Action:** Add "cozy" decoration theme. Add lighting system (warm → cool affects customer mood).

### 5. "Food delivery boom" 🛵
**Trend:** GrabFood, ShopeeFood, BeFoods still growing. Café that does delivery well wins.
**Action:** Add delivery platform integration. Add "delivery rider" character. Delivery commissions affect margins.
